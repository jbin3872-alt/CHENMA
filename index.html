About Xi'an Chenma

Xi'an Chenma Materials Co., Ltd. has been a leading provider of professional material handling solutions since 2011. Strategically located in Xi’an—a key logistics hub in Northwest China—we specialize in the design, sales, and service of a wide range of equipment including industrial trolleys, electric forklifts, intelligent stair climbers, and lifting platforms.

Our products are CE-certified and exported to over 50 countries including North America, Europe, and the Middle East. We offer flexible cooperation models such as ODM and OEM, providing end-to-end support from custom development to global shipment.
