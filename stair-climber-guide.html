How to Safely Move a 400kg Load Up Stairs

Moving heavy equipment or furniture up flights of stairs is one of the most challenging tasks in logistics. For loads up to 400kg, manual labor is not only inefficient but dangerous. This is where the Chenma LP800-1 Electric Stair Climber comes in.

Real Product Spotlight: LP800-1

Technical Specifications

  • Max Load: 400 kg
  • Battery: 48V / 72AH Lithium
  • Power: 1200 W Motor
  • Material: Manganese Steel
  • Certification: CE Certified

Core Advantages

The LP800-1 features high-strength manganese steel construction and a powerful brushless motor. Its anti-skid function on slopes ensures absolute safety for both the operator and the cargo during transport on cement or tile stairs.

Safety Checklist

  • Check Battery Level: Ensure the 48V indicator shows a full charge before starting.
  • Secure the Cargo: Always use the safety straps provided with the LP800-1.
  • Incline Angle: Maintain a 45-degree tilt for optimal balance while climbing.
  • Surface Check: Ensure stairs are clear of debris and within the height limit of 20cm per step.
